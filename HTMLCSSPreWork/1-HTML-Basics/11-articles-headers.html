Start a Fall Herb Garden

Planting an herb garden is an excellent way for beginners to develop a green thumb. Herbs are easy to grow, and many varieties take just a few weeks to fully mature from seed to harvest. Plus, you’ll save money by not having to buy fresh herbs from the grocery store. Follow the directions in the blog post on polywood.com to learn how to start a fall herb garden from scratch and which plants are best to grow this season. This helpful guide gives several options for the type of Herb Garden you might like to start.

  • Culinary Herb Garden
  • Medicinal Herb Garden
  • Scented Herb Garden
  • Ornamental Herb Garden
  • Indoor Herb Garden
  • Raised Bed Herb Garden

As you can see there are many types of Herb Gardens. There are also many varieties of herbs with which to fill your garden space.
